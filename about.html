WHO WE ARE

About Swayam

Swayam (स्वतः) means "Self" in Sanskrit. We are a non-commercial platform dedicated to helping individuals of all ages discover their inner self, find peace, and grow spiritually.

Our Vision

To create a world where everyone has access to spiritual wisdom and practices for inner peace and self-realization.

Our Mission

To provide guidance, resources, and community for spiritual growth through meditation, yoga, retreats, and authentic teachings.

Our Values

Non-commercial, authentic, inclusive, and dedicated to genuine spiritual development without dogma or commercialization.

OUR JOURNEY

The Story of Swayam

Swayam was born from a simple yet profound realization: in our fast-paced modern world, people are increasingly disconnected from their inner selves. Material success often comes at the cost of spiritual well-being, leaving many feeling empty despite external achievements.

Our founders, inspired by ancient Indian wisdom and their own spiritual journeys, envisioned a platform that would make authentic spiritual teachings accessible to everyone—free from commercialization and dogma.

Starting with small meditation sessions and gradually growing into a comprehensive platform, Swayam now serves thousands of seekers on their journey to self-discovery. From our peaceful retreat center at Yana Farm in Nashik to our online community, we continue to grow while staying true to our core mission: helping people meet their true selves.
